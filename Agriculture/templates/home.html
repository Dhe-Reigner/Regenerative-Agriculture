Regenerative agriculture

This is a farming method that aims to improve the health of the soil, water, and climate, while also reducing the negative impact of agriculture on the planet.

Principles of generative agriculture include:
  • Reducing Soil Disturbunce
  • Soil Coverage
  • Maximization of Root Life
  • Crop Diversification
  • Livestock Integration

It's an evolution of conventional farming, and it's based on the idea that the land, water, and nutrients should be able to regenerate themselves.
